How does "Find Related" work?

This feature uses a powerful ranking algorithm called Reciprocal Rank Fusion (RRF) to intelligently combine the results from two different search methods.

Think of it like searching for a restaurant. You might use a review app that ranks by taste (like our Semantic Search, which understands meaning) and a map app that ranks by distance (like our Keyword Search, which matches words). RRF is the smart process that looks at both ranked lists to find the restaurant that is both highly-rated and nearby.

It works by looking at the rank (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.) of each item in both search results. An item scores highly if it appears near the top of either list. This means a great conceptual match won't get buried just because it's missing a keyword, and vice-versa. The result is a single, superior list that gives you deeper and more accurate insights. üß†
